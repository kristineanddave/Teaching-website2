About Me...

I grew up in Armidale, Australia where I also attended university. I knew that I wanted to be a teacher ever since the third grade when I was inspired by my first male teacher. Since then, I have aspired to be a positive role model just like him. I have always taken on leadership responsibilities; whether it be captaining representative football teams, coaching football and basketball teams, or attaining leadership roles at work. I have almost completed my Masters degree where I am specialising in educational leadership. I started my teaching career in Australia and have since gained further experience teaching abroad in England and Bahrain.
Beyond teaching, I love sports, particularly recreational ones that can be pursued by individuals throughout life. I have a passion for soccer and have played at a representative level since I was eleven. I have a strong interest in health and well-being. During 2012, I obtained both a Certificate III and Certificate IV in Fitness. I am a qualified personal trainer and use my skills in fitness to assist children in developing and maintaining healthy lifestyle habits. I also enjoy travelling. I love immersing in different cultures, experiencing new things, and taking a step outside my comfort zone.
